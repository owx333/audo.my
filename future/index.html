结构选择Structure

要强制，还是要弹性Discipline vs flexibility

不是比谁回报高——是这笔钱能不能被动用、到点能不能拿到。 Not about the highest return — about whether the money gets spent early, and whether it arrives on time.

银行灵活但容易被动用；储蓄保换的是纪律与用途锁定——不是保证更高回报。提前提取 / 断缴通常会伤本金，报价时会摊开说明。 Banks are flexible but easy to raid. Savings policies buy discipline and purpose-lock — not a promised higher return. Early surrender / lapses usually hurt capital; we’ll show that at quotation.
